  • SUMMARY

    I am a second-year (final semester) MS student and RA at SeoulTech, South Korea. I am considering applying for Ph.D. studies beginning from the Fall 2021 semester and seeking a research position in a lab/research group. My research interests are: computer vision, machine learning, graphics, and related areas.

    I am currently working on computer vision with machine learning and graphics, for a fashion clothing-based virtual try-on project. We are exploring and proposing state-of-the-art for image-based and 3D model-based virtual try-on approaches. Our two extended abstracts are presented at a CVPRW 2020. We also won the 2nd place in the multi-pose image-based virtual try-on challenge in CVPR 2020.

    My prospective MS thesis title is "A Study on 3D reconstruction from clothing image and application to Virtual Try-On". I am developing hybrid methods for the virtual try-on task, combining deep neural networks and 3D modeling-based approaches. Our one full manuscript is accepted for publication in Asian Conference in Computer Vision (ACCV) 2020, titled "CloTH-VTON: Clothing Three-dimensional reconstruction for Hybrid image-based Virtual Try-ON". I also published one international journal paper from my BSc thesis, based on healthcare technologies.
